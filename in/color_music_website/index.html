Who we are.

Color Piano is the beginning of a new approach to music education. Our unusual application of colors is designed to make the learning process meaningful and interesting. The tutorials on our website introduce the concepts and tools you’ll need to understand music theory, while our app provides a fun way to practice what you’ve learned. We soon hope to offer more features in the app and more content in the tutorials. Your suggestions regarding these developments are greatly appreciated and can be sent through the form on the right. Our goal is to provide you with an engaging way to learn piano. We’re just getting started.
