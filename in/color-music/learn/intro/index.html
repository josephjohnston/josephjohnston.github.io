Tutorials

The color piano app is used to visualize and experience music through the unifying medium of color. This tutorial is designed to help you understand the theory behind the music you are playing. First, we will introduce the meaning of these colors. Then, we will explore traditional musical concepts, such as intervals, scales, and chords, in unique ways. We hope that this resource will help to further your understanding and appreciation for what you play. We’d love to hear your feedback and ideas. To talk to us, click 'Contact' on the homepage.
